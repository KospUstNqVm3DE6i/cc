Indie Assault is a love letter to your favorite fighting games, with iconic characters fighting in dynamic multiplayer battles.

! Notice that Indie Assault requires the use of a DualShock 3 controller or Xbox 360 gamepad. The game does have custom controls, but at launch the Keyboard inputs are not universally working.

Smash your friends to oblivion in Indie Assault on Steam! Our games features simple, intuitive controls, allowing us to provide fast-paced, accessible gameplay for competitive and casual players alike. Indie Assault will initially launch with a diverse roster of original and licensed indie characters. With entertaining game modes, the ability to play locally with up to 4-players, and an in-depth training mode. Indie Assault provides you with a great platform fighting game experience!

Key Features-

* A diverse initial roster of 4 characters, featuring stars from Freedom Planet, Megatroid, and the upcoming fighting game Ascension X!

* More original and licensed characters to be added as the community grows

* "Burst" – a unique new way to extend ledge play

– A strategic mechanic that is able to reset your double jump or enable you to break out of a combo

* "Blitz Assault" a comeback mechanic that enables the characters to utilize their ultimate ability

* Multiple game modes for competitive and casual play, with more to be added upon community request

* Detailed and diverse stages originating from each character’s universe

* Up to 4 local players, in any configuration imaginable!

* Training Mode with extensive customization options

* Quick thinking AI to play in addition to or instead of others

* Steam Achievements!

^ Are acquired, when you unlock in-game Medals that have stat boosts
