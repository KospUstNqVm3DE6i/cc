3D Action first-person shooter in a post-apocalyptic world.

Zombies, anomalies, huge maps, intense realtime battles!

Right now there are 5 play modes available – team deathmatch(TDM), deathmatch(DM), capture the flag (CTF), zombie mode (ZM) and peace mode.

A lot of maps available for players – starting with small ones for quick fights to the big areas where vehicles give you chance to fight your opponents on ground, water and air.



15 maps are available at this moment.
