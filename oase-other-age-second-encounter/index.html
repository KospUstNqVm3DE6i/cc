OASE – Other Age Second Encounter is a game with elements of Puzzle, Adventure Game, Dating Sim, and Visual Novel. In OASE, you control the Prince of Zeiva Empire in his quest to date a girl using a machine called Luv Luv Nitarou Dynamite. With it, you can summon girls from any place, time or space. Follow stories of different characters and collect as many endings as you can!

There are a total of 10 girls to summon, 200+ original illustrations, and 60 endings to unlock for 20+ hours worth of gameplay.

The newer version now comes with difficulty settings. If the game is too challenging, you can set it to easy mode and defeat the game with ease.
