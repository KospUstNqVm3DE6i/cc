Overcast : Walden and the Werewolf is a Western Survival Horror game developed by Microblast Games.

The game tells the story of Walden, an old hunter who lives alone in the woods. Recently, Walden began to be haunted by a mysterious creature that destroyed the village located near his cabin, killing every person residing on the premises. Now, Walden wants revenge. In order to accomplish this, the old hunter will hunt the beast and adventure in numerous horrifying scenarios that only an horror classic can provide.
